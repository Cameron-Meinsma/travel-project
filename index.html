Why Los Angeles?

Los Angeles, home of the entertainment industry

Hollywood, which is a neighborhood in LA, is the epicentre of the entertainment industry. With a tantamount of blockbuster movies, award winning tv shows, and record breaking songs under its belt, LA is home to lots of world-famous celebrities. A lot of celebrities, especially A-listers, live in Beverly Hills, which is a suburb in LA. Beverly Hills has the famous 90210 postal code. Some celebrities own a beach house in Malibu, which is part of the LA county.
